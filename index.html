about

"A Tuesday" is the result of an intense fascination with the "choose your own" concept and how it shows up in different mediums; specifically video games, comics, and novels. So after I tumbled down the rabbit hole that is the structure of Edward Packard's classic "Choose your own adventure" novels, getting stuck in the loop of George Wylesol's 2120, and playing one too many visual novel pc games... I created the most mundane click thru comic (that also started out as a zine). Phew ok hope u enjoyed :-)
